Cafeterías bonitas en Murcia

Paradise Gastro Bar

A favorite among locals, Paradise Gastro Bar offers a unique open-air patio and great coffee. It’s a perfect spot to unwind with friends.

Socolá

A cozy spot to enjoy delicious coffee and desserts with a welcoming atmosphere.

Glea

A modern cafe with stylish decor and a fantastic selection of specialty coffees and desserts.

Cafe Milano

La Mesa Cafe is famous for its minimalist interior and excellent brunch options.

Verdementa

A hidden gem offering a variety of unique coffee blends and aromatic teas.

Cafe Alba

Blending art and coffee culture, Cafe Alba features exhibitions and a creative environment.

Vértigo y Calambre

Known for its delicious hot chocolate and cozy ambiance, it’s a great spot for a relaxed afternoon.

Drexco

A delightful spot for those with a sweet tooth, offering homemade cakes and pastries.

Mariantonietta Cafe

An eco-friendly cafe with a wide selection of plant-based options and organic coffee.
